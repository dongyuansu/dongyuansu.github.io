Dongyuan Su

Master Supervisor · School of Artificial Intelligence, SZTU

Dongyuan Su received his Bachelor's degree in June 2019 from the Special Class of High Performance Computing (SCHPC) of the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering at Shenzhen University. He obtained his Ph.D. in December 2024 from Shenzhen University (supervised by Prof. Laizhong Cui). His research focuses on data transmission, collaborative learning, and collaborative inference mechanisms in vehicular networks.

He has published 6 papers in international journals and conferences such as TSC, ICNP, TMM, TITS, MM, and CN, and has been granted 1 national invention patent. He serves as a reviewer for journals such as TON, TMM, and CN.
